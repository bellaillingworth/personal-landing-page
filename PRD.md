
# Product Requirement Document

## 01 — Problem Statement

Bella Illingworth is a Business Analytics and Information Systems student at the University of Iowa graduating in May 2026. She is seeking Business Analyst roles after graduation and is open to working across industries, with a preference for positions in cities in the United States or Europe.

While her resume outlines her experience and skills, it does not fully showcase her analytical work, visual projects, and design abilities. Recruiters and hiring managers often scan resumes quickly, making it difficult to demonstrate the depth of her work in analytics, dashboards, and product-oriented projects.

The purpose of the personal landing page is to provide a clear and visual representation of Bella’s experience, projects, and technical skills. The page will allow recruiters and hiring managers to quickly understand her capabilities while also highlighting her ability to present data insights and projects in a visually engaging way.

---

## 02 — Target Audience

The primary visitors to the landing page are professionals involved in hiring for analytics and product-related roles.

Primary audience:
- Recruiters at tech companies and startups
- Hiring managers for analytics and product teams
- Product managers evaluating analytical talent

These visitors typically want to quickly understand a candidate’s background, skills, and project experience. When arriving on the landing page, they should be able to quickly determine who Bella is, what skills she has, and what projects demonstrate her ability to analyze data and generate insights.

---

## 03 — Goals & Success Metrics

| Goal | How We Measure It |
|-----|-----|
| Increase visibility with recruiters and hiring managers | 100+ page visits within 30–60 days |
| Generate recruiter engagement | At least 3 recruiter emails referencing the site |
| Convert visibility into job opportunities | At least 3 interviews within 30–60 days |
